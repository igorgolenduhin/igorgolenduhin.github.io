Gift Recomendations

Bark box

Have you ever though about presenting your dog with all delicous things they like?

Then this box is for you. You will receive every month a themed box with treats and toys for your lovelly furry. The amount of treats you receive is based on the choosed subscription and the toys are related to your dog characteristcs.

Godfather

Have you ever though about going out with your dog with the same outfit as you?

This store may have the solution for you. Dogfather offers a variety of clothes for dogs going from fashion dresses to tiny ribbons.

Pet Smart

How am I going to clean my pet's teeth if every time I get near them, he tries to bite me?

If you have a problem like that or just want to know how to clean your dog's teeth, this tip is for you. Did you know you can reduce the vet bills just by giving him dental treats. There is a variety of types and prices based on your dog's preference. In this website you can find a variety of itens for your pet going from regular cable to advanced veterinary nutrition.

Petvalu

Is your dog your best friend? Would you like to dress him up for the festivities such as Halloween of Christmas?

This store offers pet costumes that will make your pet be the attention wherever you decide to go with him. This store like Petsmart also offers a variety of products for your loved one.

Furbo Dog Camera

Would like to keep your dog entertained while you are outside?

Dog Furbo Camera provides the ability of tossing treats for your dog while you are outside your home and is also a camera and audio to see how your pet is doing. Maybe he likes to sleep or to chew the corner of your sofa, this is the way for discovering it.

PetChatz

Are you a technology lover? Would you like to use an all-in-one gadget to make your life easier?

PetChatz is a two way camera and audio that your dog can interact with you, watch dog tv, and it also has calming aromatherapy. It alsos has the functionality of throwing treats while playing games with your dog. Check it out if you are a gadget fun and want to reduce your dog's anxiety problem.

Seat Cover

Are you planning to take your pet on a road trip?

This tip is for you. This is a dog seat cover that will protect your car against dirty or spills and will make your dog confortable and safe during the ride.
