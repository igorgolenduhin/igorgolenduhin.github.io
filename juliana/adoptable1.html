Lucy is a survivor from the huricane Harvey. She was rescued after this phenomenon devasted her home city. It is unknown if she had a family before of if she was born as a stray dog. She has been with us ever since. Recently she started living with a foster mom who has considerable compliments about her.

Lucy is a shy dog but she is very lovely. She loves strangers even at her foster home and she gets along with the foster cat Eric. She has no problems with anxiety and stay at home sleeping or playing with her toys for some time if needed. She would be more suitable in a house without toddlers as she gets scared around them because of her size and starts barking. She loves to play with other dogs. She would be great for a family looking to have more than one pet.

If you want to know more about her or book a visit, please fell free to reach us.
