Adoptables

Ryker is an approximately 2.5 year old female Rotweiler found as a stray in Northern Manitoba. He arrived to us a few days ago and we are still learning about him. What we do know about him is that he absolutely adores people. His favourite thing to do is give hugs when you sit with him to let you know how much he's enjoying your company! Ryker did not have the best start to life, but he does not let that bring him down.

He is good with people and dogs but not cat tested. He is over 100lbs so would benefit from an owner experienced with stronger/larger breeds. Ryker would do best in a home where his owners are around more often than not. He will also need a detached home with no shared walls as he can get a bit vocal when left alone.

If you want to know more about her or book a visit, please fell free to reach us.
