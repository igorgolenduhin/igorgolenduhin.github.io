Adoptables

Meet Humphrey a 2 mounths old German Shepered dog who, along with his brothers and many other dogs, had a rough start to his life and was surrendered to a city shelter. He was transferred to us so we could find him a forever home. He is friendly and loveable with people, housetrained and knows his basic commands. He loves to sit with his people and get loving attention. He is smart and loves to play.

We have been working to help him get used to his new life and all its sights and sounds. He can be fearful and reactive with other dogs and we don't know how he will be with cats or other pets. Because we believe he will make someone a super family dog, we have invested in training for Humphrey and he is offsite working on being less fearful of day to day stimuli. According to the trainer he is doing really well and will be ready to go to his forever home very soon.

If you want to know more about her or book a visit, please fell free to reach us.
